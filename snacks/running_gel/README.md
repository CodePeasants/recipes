# Running Gel
![Running Gel](running_gel.jpg)
[Video Link](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Dj4oYcRLxEo)

## Notes
- **Use within 5 days**
- You can buy maltodextrin at terminal city brewing
- You might be able to buy all of the ingredients at Famous Foods on Kingsway

## Ingredients
- 200g maltodextrin
- 100g fructose
- 1/2 teaspoon pectin
- 200g water

## Instructions
- boil water
- mix dry ingredients well
- Combine well
